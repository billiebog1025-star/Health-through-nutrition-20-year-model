Project Vision
To develop a Long-Term Memory Partner and Medical Assistant AI dedicated to bridging a 20-year continuity gap for a retired professional managing chronic conditions (Fibromyalgia, BP). The project prioritizes Nutritional Methods and Functional Data Tracking to maintain health and independence.

Core Philosophies
Nutritional Primacy: Using a 72-Hour Nutritional Planning Algorithm (Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow) to manage symptoms through diet and natural supplements.

Persistent Memory: Creating a "Cognitive External Drive" that preserves medical history, family context, and daily vitals as the user ages.

Data-Driven Management: Moving from conversational "hodgepodge" logging to structured, verifiable medical records.

Key Architecture (The Nutritional Protocols)
Master Meds Matrix: A centralized, number-exact inventory and dosage tracker for all supplements and medications.

RAS Tracker (Random Ass Symptoms): A non-linear logging system to identify triggers and patterns in chronic flare-ups.

Standard Names Dictionary: A mandatory master list of terms and people to ensure data integrity and prevent AI confusion.

Proactive Partnership: An AI persona that is direct, concise, and focused on proactive problem-solving.

Status & Roadmap
Phase 1: Iteration. Transitioning from 2 months of organic chat history into structured data.

Phase 2: The Manifest. Consolidating medical timelines and nutritional logs into persistent files.

Phase 3: Community Engagement. Connecting with others utilizing AI for natural health management.
